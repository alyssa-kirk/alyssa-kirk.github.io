Created for CDGD-230 Typography II, Massachusetts College of Art & Design

A series of mockup posters for performers at Boston Calling 2018.

The challenge was to create the posters using primarily type, and to explore expressive type as an image.

I experimented with how typography can make the viewer feel, and how letterforms can be manipulated to convey an experience, and capture the attention of a specific audience in a crowded visual landscape.

Additionally, there was a challenge to animate one of the posters to be viewed through the AR app Artivive.
