Created for CDGD-230 Typography II, Massachusetts College of Art & Design

A redesign of A Book Apart's Design is a Job by Mike Monteiro.

I redesigned the entire printed work from the book jacket to the text block. I then printed and handbound the book myself to create a finished product.

I used typography to create a hierarchical system that was used throughout the book. Every page was designed on the same grid to pull it all together cohesively.
